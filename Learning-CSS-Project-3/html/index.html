CSS Final Project

What's this all about now?

This is a very simple series of pages that are used as a means of practicing HTML and CSS. In particulary, I'm trying to practice with grids and the box model.

There's nothing complicated here, but the links in the navigation bar at the top will take you to a styled page displaying some pictures from two public domain websites.

One of the websites, The Gender Spectrum, shows some pictures of non-binary and trans folks. The other, Disabled and Here shows some pictures and illustrations of folks with disabilities.
